GENERAL INFORMATION

The admission of pupils is subject to availability and according to the criteria fixed by the Seth Juggilal Poddar Academy (age, school documents...) The pupil must be introduced by his/her father, mother, or legal guardian, who must provide all the documents listed in the admission form. Seth Juggilal Poddar Academy offers admissions to Playgroup, Nursery, Junior Kindergarten, Senior Kindergarten and classes I onwards.

ADMISSION POLICY

The admission process is as follows:

Admissions are given subject to fulfillment of admission criteria and availability of seats, without any reference to caste, creed, class, gender or political inclination. Admission is a privilege and not a right. Rights of admission are reserved with the school management-and the school management can accept or reject any admission without assigning any reason. Affix and attach the necessary documents specified in the Admission Form. ADMISSION PROCESS: THERE ARE TWO STAGES IN THE ADMISSION PROCESS. Online Process Offline Process.

Stage I : Online Process

Visit www.sjpoddaracademy.edu.in. Click on "Admission Process". Fill in the General Information Form with full details. Upload all the original documents listed in the Admission Information Click on "Submit" Once the form is approved the "Admission list of the confirmed students for the Year 2020- 2021 will be displayed on the School Notice Board" on December 23, 2019 latest by 5.00pm.

Stage II : Offline Process

After confirmation, visit the school office to collect the Admission Form with prospectus onthe stipulated date and time. (The cost of the Admission Form is Rs.500 which is non-refundable). Fill in the General Information Form with full details. Submit all the uploaded original documents for verification at the time of admission with one photo copy self attested. Originals will be returned to you after Verification. (Documents required are listed on the Admission Information on page.2) All the necessary information regarding the Admission will be provided at the School Office.( Office Timings: 10.30 am to 1.00 pm)

DOCUMENTS TO BE SUBMITTED AT THE TIME OF ADMISSION PROCESS

Original Birth Certificate. For certificates in Languages other than English, please bring a notarized copy of the birth certificate translated in English. Proof of Residence must be in the name of either parent (Any two of the following documents can be produced as a proof of residence). Valid Passport. Current Electricity Bill. CurrentTelephone Bill. Registered Agreement Copy (in case of rental or leased apartment). Ration Card Copy.
Aadhaar Card of Father, Mother and Student (Compulsory). Original School Leaving Certificate from recognized School with correct School UDISE number and Student ID number. Photocopy of Progress/ Report Card of the Previous School. (Filling of the form is not a confirmation of Admission.)

ADMISSION CONFIRMATION

Admission will be confirmed only after:

  1. Approval / Confirmation of Admission.
  2. Paying the stipulated fees, as mentioned within the specified time period.
  3. Completion of required documents as listed (In case Transfer Certificate cannot be produced at the time of admission, it should be submitted latest before the school starts).

ACADEMIC CALENDAR Seth Juggilal Poddar Academy (ICSE) follows an academic calendar of April to March with the summer vacation in April end to June. The school has a fortnight long Diwali vacation in October/November and another fortnight long Christmas vacation in December/January. This academic calender is indicative and is subject to change.
